To install, put the compiled .dll, the mod.json and LightningTexture.png file into a subfolder inside the "Mods" folder.
Example structure:
Blade & Sorcery\Mods\ScriptingExample4\BASScriptingExample4.dll
Blade & Sorcery\Mods\ScriptingExample4\mod.json
Blade & Sorcery\Mods\ScriptingExample4\LightningTexture.png
